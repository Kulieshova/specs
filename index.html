What is SPECS?

Students Persuing Exporation of Computer Science is a new club at Masters that combines computer programming and social service. In our club, we will teach you how to code, design social apps, and how to implement them. Not to mention, that our members will be able to present their pragrams at various competitions, hackathons, and conferences -- and, of course, these programs will actually help people.

Are you still unsure whether the club will be a great choce for you? Just try it! You're free to leave any moment (but believe me, after seeing how cool the club is, you will not want to do this!;))
